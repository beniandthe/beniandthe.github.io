What happens in beta

Browse, like, verify, apply.

Woofer’s current beta centers on four things: cleaner pet data, lighter UX, stronger verification, and more reliable rescue handoff. We’re building the full concept web-first before mobile follows.

  • Cleaner LA-area pet feed and better cover-photo selection
  • Identity verification and fuller rescue-ready application packets
  • Optional one-time payments and support flows instead of subscriptions
  • Human-centered polish before public publish branches
